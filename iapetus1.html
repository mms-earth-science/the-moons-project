Geology

The atmostphere of Iapetus is unique. One hemisphere is bright, while the other is dark as coal. The dark material could've been from another dark moon near Iapetus. The seperation of the two colors are caused by the Sun's heat being absorbed in the dark material and the light elements reflecting the radiation.

The geology of Iapetus is three quarters ice and one quarter rock. There is a large mountain chain along the edge of the two hemispheres that reaches 6 miles high.

The Yin Yang of Saturn's Moons

Iapetus has an atmosphere that is partially dark and partially light. Giovanni Cassini found that you could only see Iapetus on the west side of Saturn, showing that one side is darker and less visible than the other.
